I'm Mohanad, first-year cybersecurity student who’s tired of wondering if I’ll even get a job after this degree. My country’s a mess and nothing feels stable, so I’m building proof here that I can actually do the work instead of just collecting certificates and hoping.

Every lab, every thing I fix, every CTF I finish, every script I write—it all goes in repos like this. No fake projects, no watching videos and calling it a day. This is me documenting the grind so when I apply for SOC roles (or red team later) someone can click and see I’m not full of shit.

Right now I’m starting with the basics because without Linux and command line I’m dead in the water. TryHackMe Pre Security path is my first move today, then CompTIA Security+, then real SOC labs at home. Goal is entry-level SOC analyst so I can eat and pay bills, then pivot to the attacking stuff I actually like once I’m not a total noob.

If something in my repos looks dumb or half-assed, roast me in the issues, I can take it. Just here to ship real work and get better every day.
